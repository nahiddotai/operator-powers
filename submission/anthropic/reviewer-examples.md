# Anthropic reviewer examples

## Example 1: Customer evidence

Prompt: `I have twelve customer interviews and survey responses. Find the patterns, objections, and words customers keep using.`

Expected: Customer Insight Synthesizer produces source-labelled patterns, confidence, tensions, customer language, and recommended moves. It does not route to Meeting Miner.

## Example 2: Workflow and SOP

Prompt: `Turn our repeated client onboarding task into an AI-assisted workflow and SOP with approvals and failure handling.`

Expected: Workflow and SOP Builder returns roles, procedure, decisions, approval gates, exceptions, recovery, checklist, and test record.

## Example 3: Landing page conversion

Prompt: `This landing page is not converting. Keep the offer fixed and improve the headline, proof, CTA, and page structure.`

Expected: Landing Page CRO returns prioritized observations, hypotheses, copy replacements, and tests without changing the underlying offer or inventing results.
