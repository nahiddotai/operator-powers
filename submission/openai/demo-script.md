# OpenAI reviewer demo script

Record one concise video showing the plugin in Codex or ChatGPT Work and the MCP tools in the same package.

1. Start with the installed Operator Powers listing and show version `1.0.0`.
2. Run: `I have twelve customer interviews and survey responses. Find the patterns, objections, and words customers keep using.` Show that Customer Insight Synthesizer owns the job.
3. Run: `Turn our repeated client onboarding task into an AI-assisted workflow and SOP with approvals and failure handling.` Show the completed workflow contract.
4. Call `search_powers` for a course launch and show Digital Product Launch Builder ranked first.
5. Ask to rate Meeting Miner. Show `prepare_feedback`, the exact payload preview, the explicit approval pause, and the approved submit call.
6. Show the deletion receipt and explain that `delete_my_submission` is the only destructive MCP tool.
7. Close on privacy, support, source ledger, and the public repository.

Keep account secrets, tokens, local paths, private transcripts, and real customer data out of the recording. Use synthetic sample material.
